📄 Required Documents (Sightseeing Visa)

1. Visa application form
2. Passport photo (white background)
3. Passport (original + copy)
4. Entry/Exit certificate (NIA Taiwan)
5. ARC (original + copy)
6. Re-entry permit
7. Certificate of enrollment
8. Bank balance proof
9. Travel itinerary
10. Flight booking
11. Hotel reservation

🎯 Special Cases

Attending Conference:
+ Invitation letter from Japan

Visiting Relatives:
+ Residence certificate in Japan
+ Invitation letter

⚠️ Important Notes

• Use A4 paper only
• Do NOT staple documents
• No pencil or erasable pen
• Additional documents may be required
