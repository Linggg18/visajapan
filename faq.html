Frequently Asked Questions

1. How long does visa processing take?
Usually 5–7 working days, depending on your documents.
2. Do I need to go in person?
Yes, applicants must submit documents directly to the office.
3. Can I use printed or digital documents?
Only printed A4 documents are accepted.
4. What if my documents are incomplete?
Your application may be delayed or rejected.
5. Where do I apply?
You can apply in Taipei or Kaohsiung office depending on your ARC address.
