📄 Required Documents

1. Visa application form
2. Passport photo (white background)
3. Passport (original + copy)
4. Entry/Exit certificate
5. ARC (original + copy)
6. Re-entry permit
7. Certificate of enrollment
8. Bank statement
9. Travel schedule
10. Flight booking
11. Hotel reservation

🧭 Step by Step Process

Step 1: Prepare all documents
Step 2: Fill visa application form
Step 3: Go to Taipei or Kaohsiung office
Step 4: Submit documents
Step 5: Wait for approval

⚠️ Important Notes

• Use A4 paper only
• Do NOT staple documents
• No pencil or erasable pen
• Additional documents may be required
