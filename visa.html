Frequently Asked Questions

1. How long does visa processing take?
Usually 5–7 working days depending on documents.
2. Do I need to go in person?
Yes, applicants must submit documents directly to the office.
3. Can I submit digital documents?
No, only printed A4 documents are accepted.
4. What if my documents are incomplete?
Your application may be delayed or rejected.
5. Where do I apply?
At Taipei or Kaohsiung office depending on your ARC address.
