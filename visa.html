📄 Required Documents

1. Visa application form
2. Passport photo (white background)
3. Passport (original + copy)
4. Entry/Exit certificate
5. ARC (if applicable)
6. Bank statement
7. Travel itinerary
8. Flight booking
9. Hotel reservation

🧭 Step by Step Process

Step 1: Prepare documents
Step 2: Fill application form
Step 3: Go to embassy office
Step 4: Submit documents
Step 5: Wait for approval

⚠️ Important Notes

• Use A4 paper only
• Do NOT staple documents
• No pencil or erasable pen
• Extra documents may be requested
