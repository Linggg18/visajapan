Japan Visa Guide

Basic information about applying for a Japan visa from Taiwan.

📌 Overview

Japan visa is required for students and travelers in Taiwan who plan to enter Japan for tourism, study, or visiting purposes.

🧭 Basic Process

1. Prepare documents
2. Fill visa application form
3. Submit at Japan-Taiwan office
4. Wait for approval
5. Receive visa result

📄 Common Requirements

• Passport
• Application form
• Photo (recent)
• Financial proof
• Travel plan (if tourism)

⚠️ Important Notes

• Apply in person
• Documents must be printed (A4)
• Processing time varies
• Additional documents may be requested
